Cheese Pizza

Description

To make a cheese pizza, you need pizza dough, tomato sauce, and cheese. Roll out the dough, add tomato sauce, sprinkle either shredded mozzarella, parmesan, or cheddar cheese, and bake in the oven with optional toppings for 12-15 minutes.

Use high-quality ingredients like fresh pizza dough, a variety of cheeses, and different tomato sauces to add depth of flavor. Experiment with unique toppings like fresh herbs to create a tasty and flavorful cheese pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomatoe sauce
  • Shredded cheese:
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
    • Cheddar
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 230Â°C
  2. Roll out the pizza dough into a circular shape and place it on a baking sheet or pizza stone
  3. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the pizza dough, leaving a small border around the edges
  4. Sprinkle a generous amount of any shredded cheese we adviced you to buy (or a combination of all of them)
  5. Drizzle a smaal amount of olive oil over the pizza to help it cook evenly
  6. Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly
  7. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cook for a few minutes before slicing and serving
