Cheese Empanadas

Description

Cheese empanadas are a pastry filled with cheese and spices. The dough can be made or purchased, and the filling can include different cheeses and add-ins. They are baked until crispy, making for a delicious snack or appetizer.

To make cheese empanadas, mix shredded cheese with spices, fill empanada dough, and bake until golden brown. Enjoy hot as a tasty snack or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • Empanada dough (can be either store-bought or homemade)
  • Shredded cheese (such as cheddar, mozzarella, or a combination)
  • Spices (such as cumin, paprika and powder)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Water (for sealing the empanadas)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190Â°C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Roll out the empanada dough and cut into circles using a cookie cutter or a small bowl
  3. Mix together the shredded cheese, spices, salt, and pepper in a bowl
  4. Spoon a small amount of the cheese mixture onto each empanada dough circle
  5. Use a small amount of water to moistein the edges of the dough circle
  6. Fold the dough over the cheese filling to create a half moon shape
  7. Use a fork to crimp the edges of the dough to seal the filling inside
  8. Place the empanadas on the prepared baking sheet
  9. Brush the tops of the empanadas with beaten egg or milk for a golden crust
  10. Bake the empanadas for 15-20 minutes or until the dough is goldden brown and crispy
  11. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy hot as a delicious snack or appetizer
